Who We Are

About MentorHub

As a premium educational institute ecosystem, The Trajectory Academy (MentorHub) is dedicated to providing bulletproof quality education to students of all ages and backgrounds. We believe in nurturing a deep-seated love for structured learning and equipping students with the conceptual toolsets they need to succeed in their academic journeys through interactive, personalized 1-on-1 private home tutoring.

The academy was established by Dr. (Mrs.) Apeksha Gupta, who holds a Ph.D. in Chemical Engineering from NIT Raipur, and has served as a post-doctoral fellow at IIT Guwahati. With extensive, long-term experience in high-tier technical teaching and scientific research backed by 10+ SCI publications, she is an active member of both the Indian Institute of Chemical Engineers and the Institution of Engineers.

Our burning passion to set up a strong, unwavering foundation for today's generation gave birth to this platform in 2020. Our main motive remains centered around building strong, clear learning channels at your absolute ease with optimized offline coaching and tailored group classes at the academy.
